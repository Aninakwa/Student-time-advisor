Welcome to Time Advisor

Time Advisor is a Pomodoro‑based productivity tool to help you schedule, track, and complete tasks. Use the sidebar to navigate between your Dashboard, History, Settings, Profile, and this Help page.

Getting Started
  • Sign Up / Log In: Use email/password or Google/Facebook social login.
  • Dashboard: View your tasks for today, grouped by due date.
  • Add Task: Click + Add Task to create a new task with title, details, durations, cycles, and schedule time.
Creating & Managing Tasks
  • Edit Task: Click Edit next to a task to change its settings.
  • Delete Task: Remove a task permanently with the Delete button.
  • Start Task: Launch the Pomodoro timer for a task; focus intervals and breaks cycle automatically.
  • Cycles: Determines how many work+break loops you’ll do before the task is marked complete.
  • Schedule Time: If you set a time, you’ll get an alert at that time to start the task.
Using the Pomodoro Timer
  • Work Sessions: Default length set in Settings (25 min by default).
  • Breaks: Short and long break durations are configurable and trigger automatically.
  • Controls: Start, Pause/Resume, Reset buttons control the timer; progress bar and cycle counter keep you informed.
  • Motivation: Enjoy rotating motivational quotes and optional background music during sessions.
  • Notifications: Sound and browser notifications alert you when intervals end.
History

See all your tasks—completed and pending—in the History page. Use filters by status and due‑date range to find exactly what you need.

Settings

Customize your default Pomodoro lengths, enable/disable sound alerts and browser notifications, and choose your theme (light/dark) in Settings.

Profile

View and update your display name and avatar. Click the pencil icon on your photo to upload a new image directly.

Tips & Best Practices
  • Break tasks into small, focused chunks to maximize Pomodoro effectiveness.
  • Use the schedule feature to remind yourself to start important tasks.
  • Keep an eye on your History to identify patterns and optimize your work flow.
